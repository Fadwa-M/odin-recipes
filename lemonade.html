Description

Lemonade is one of my favourite drinks, especially in the hot summers we have here. The strong taste of lemons is very refreshing.

Ingredients

  • 6 lemons.
  • 2 cups of water.
  • 2 tbsp of white sugar.

Steps

    Peel the lemons and put them in the juice mixer.
    Add the water.
    Add the sugar.
    Mix until the lemons are well-blended.
    Strain the juice in a drinking glass.
